David Kolb (1939 - present). `-` an american educational theorist recognised for his work on experiental learning. <br />

Kolb's experiental learning cycle consists of: <br />

**Abstract conceptualisation** <br />
` - ` Learners learn from new ideas <br />
` - ` Learners revisit existing ideas and modify ased on reflective observation <br />

**Active Experimentation** <br />
` - ` Learners apply new ideas to surroundings <br />
` - ` Learners apply updated ideas to surroundings <br />

**Concrete experience** <br />
` - ` Learners encounter new experiences <br />
` - ` Learners reinterpret existing experiences <br />

**Reflect observation** <br />
` - ` Learners reflect on the experience <br />
