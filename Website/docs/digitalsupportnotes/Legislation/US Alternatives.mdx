US alternatives to UK acts. 

Investigatory Powers Act 2016: **ECPA**


Freedom of Information Act 2000: **Freedom of information act ([www.foia.gov](http://www.foia.gov/))**


Computer Misuse Act 1990: **Computer Fraud and Abuse Act**


Copyright, Designs and patents act 1988: **Copyright Law of the United States**


Data protection act 1998 and GDPR: **California Consumer Privacy Act, New York data privacy law...**
