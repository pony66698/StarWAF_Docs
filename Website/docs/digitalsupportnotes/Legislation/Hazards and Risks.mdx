A hazard and a risk are two different things. 

What is a hazard - A hazard is anything that has potential to cause harm. 

This could be a bare wire, trailing cable or wet floor.


What is a risk - What could happen due to the hazard. 

E.g. Trailing cable (hazard) trip over it (risk).
